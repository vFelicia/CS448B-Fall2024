in the following video you will learn how to limit your classrooms progress within a course limiting the students progress is done per classroom and it allows you to control the pacing of the students within your classroom to limit progress go to the my classrooms page and choose the classroom that you want to limit the progress for from the progress tab click on the limit progress button here you can enter the number of the challenge from which you want to limit the students progress I will enter 20 and click on save this means that your students cannot access challenges beyond challenge 20 when finishing challenge 20 they will get a message that their teacher has locked the rest of the challenges you can return your students to free play by clicking again on limit progress and choosing free play if you are using our lessons feature with coding adventure you can choose to limit progress based on the last lesson taught refer to the lessons tab for more information you can choose to limit your classrooms progress in other courses as well just locate the course on the dropdown menu at the top right corner of your screen thank you for watching this video on how to limit a classrooms progress
