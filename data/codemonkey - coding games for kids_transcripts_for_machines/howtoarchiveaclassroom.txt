in the following video you will learn how to archive a classroom on code monkey in order to archive a classroom you no longer teach go to the my classrooms page choose which classroom you want to archive and click on the archive button in the actions column there you will see the option to archive the classroom please note archiving a classroom will not free up space in your seats quota unless you have a flex subscription now click on archive and type archive to confirm once you noticed that your classroom was archived you can then click on archive and see that the classroom is there if at a later stage you would like to restore your classroom click on restore classroom and your class will be restored for information on how to archive an individual student click on the link in the video's description or visit code monkeys Help Center thank you for watching this video on how to archive a classroom on code monkey
