Explore Digital Technology learnings with our Scratch Times Table game. You can interact with the Shark Quiz Master. You can type in your name when the Shark asks you What is your name? The Shark will say we're going to play a Times Table game. As the player you can decide on HOW MANY questions you would like to try! You simply type in the number of questions so the Shark will know how many to ask you. Lots of positive praise when you get them correct If you get them wrong, the Shark will ask you to try again. After completing all your questions, you get your score!
