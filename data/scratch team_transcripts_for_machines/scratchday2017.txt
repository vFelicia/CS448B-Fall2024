So what is Scratch Day? Scratch day is a day for people around the world to meet, care and learn about Scratch. Each Scratch Day celebration is unique but they all share a playful spirit. Whether you are just getting started on Scratch, trying new materials to extend that Scratch knowledge, or collaborating with a new friend, Scratch day has something for everyone. How can you participate in Scratch Day? To attend the Scratch Day, browse the site to discover an event near you. To host a Scratch Day, you can explore the site for activity ideas and tips for planning and you can post your events for others to find. This years celebration is during the month of May which also happens to be the 10th anniversary of Scratch. But you can celebrate Scratch day Any day of the year. How will you celebrate Scratch day?
